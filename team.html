Meet the Team behind OCMii


The Future is ours

Dereck Benjamin is the former CIO and EVP/Business Development at MAERSK. Dereck Benjamin wishes to see the Caribbean region rise up the modern challenges and become the true powerhouse it is unequivocally destined to become. He is mainly concerned with helping the many small countries overcome the challenges they face today in fuel supply, financial controls, and risk management. With his global perspective, the entrepreneurial-minded Benjamin is a worldwide recognized expert in business procedures related to business economics. He has authored several publications and regularly contributes to international conferences. His career has focused on business process engineering and optimization with a highlight on developing technology in developing nations, that intelligently brings about and empowers the supply chain. As COO at GasPlus, a company he co-founded, Benjamin created supply chain management and tax automation solutions throughout the downstream energy industry for clients like Air France, Air Canada, Evergreen, Maersk, and Chevron. He launched his career with PETROChem in chemistry, eventually handling distributor development for the lubricants market.

Absalon Cardinal is on a mission to bridge the gap between the old and the new Caribbean. A native of Trinidad, he is a well-known, well-respected Merger and Acquisition specialist with a proven record of accomplishment of results in strategic planning. Do not let his youthful appearance fool you; he is a finance wizard with a focus in risk management. A merger is a complicated process, and the responsibilities of high in demand specialists. His portfolio includes Fortune 500 Companies. He is very excited to join the OCMii and is eager to put his knowledge to the development of the Caribbean region and help uncover untapped potential and resources The Caribbean has long been dominated by a colonial culture. According to Cardinal, it is high time for our region to take control of its destiny and be the lead player in the global economy. Cardinal enjoy hands on approach and his very strong in communication. His areas of expertise are Multi task management; conflict resolution, budget, financial controls and negotiation skills. WE are extremely fortunate to have him onboard.
