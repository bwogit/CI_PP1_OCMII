About

OCMii combines my knowledge of the Irish Innovation landscape
and my strong desire to export it to the Caribbean Markets. OCMii aims
to be a trusted bridge and a channel for economic partnerships between the Caribbean
market and the Irish Enterprises and Innovation network. OCMii also has strong ambitions o facilitate
on going cooperation between the all the Caribbean
Nations and the EU through Martinique and Guadeloupe which have a native visibility and access into EU's top Industry, knowhow, Innovation and
projects thanks to existing economic, political and financial, policies and agreements.

It is critical for the Caribbean nations to be active participants in the fourth
industrial revolution (new technologies). Innovation is paramount for maintaining
competitiveness in a Global Market especially for small countries.The Caribbean region
in general suffers from the emigration of its talent, which is a hindrance to
progress and a disaster when there is no indigenous replacement.Even when the
desire to come back is strong, our emigrated elite is reluctant to return home, for
lack of the comparable work and life conditions which they are accustomedto. Providing
cutting-edge innovation and projects would allow the Caribbean a to be key international player through:
• The retention of our talent by creating and maintaining high-value jobs
• Developing and nurturing businesses, scientists and talented people.
• Attracting back to our shores our elite currently based in America, Africa, Europe, and Asia.

I have the unshakable belief that the Caribbean is a market that international
businesses will have to contend with. From Cuba to Puerto Rico and from the Bahamas
to Trinidad & Tobago, a myriad of business opportunities await partners with vision.
Born and raised in Martinique (French outermost region). I possess the indigenous desire
to participate in the transformation of the Caribbean from a tourism dependant zone to a
major economic player. For the past 20 years I have lived and worked in Ireland in a highly
advanced technological field within the very competitive sector of Higher Education
Institutes (HEI). OCMii (Opening the Caribbean Market to Investment and innovation)
taps into the important “HEI - Irish Industry” synergy, which has been responsible for
the creation of successful international companies and start-ups. This synergy has been
the main contributor to the establishment of Ireland as a global leader of Innovation.
OCMii aims to become the perfect Innovation gateway into the Caribbean.
